About Company

Welcome to the about page for Gamers IQ,

Our first game we sold was Super Mario This game was for a stranger who came up to us one day when we first started the company. The stranger saw The Boss holding the game and said "How much do you want for it?" Then pointed at the game. He said he would pay us $45, because his son has always wanted that game. So we sold it to him, weeks later we got a review on our first website and it was the stranger, He said his son loved the game and he was very thankful. That was our first company sale and it was the first batch of money we got, It inspired us to do more.

We became a serious company some years later, and got back to Programming Languages. We are now starting to developing our own apps and Websites that are better than what we have now; at the moment, we're working hard on a very interesting Application.

Please feel free to contact us in case that you need anything. Hope we can sell things to you or maybe you can learn code!
